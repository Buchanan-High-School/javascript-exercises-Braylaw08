Buchanan Rodeo Club

What Will You Learn?

Rodeo club teaches you the basics of roping like learning how to swing a rope on a foot and roping dummy. You will also learn the fundemental skills for bull riding on a stationary barrel. You will also learn the basics of barrel racing.

Who Puts it on?

Hunter decided that he wanted to get high schoolers who wanted to do rodeo stuff a chance to learn how early rather than learning as an adult. Hunter started riding bulls when he got out or high school. He was doing good and rode at multiple places. Now he Ropes calves all over the country.

Where

Rodeo club is usually held in the student parking lot at the high school. The address for the high school is 401 W Chicago St, Buchanan, MI 49107.
